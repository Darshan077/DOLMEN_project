WHAT IS DOLMEN® 25 LIQUID STICK?


ABOUT THE DRUG

Dolmen® 25 liquid stick is a fast and effective pain-relieving medication indicated for symptomatic treatment of mild-to-moderate intensity pain, such as musculoskeletal pain, menstrual pain (dysmenorrhoea) and dental pain.2,3 Dexketoprofen trometamol is a pain relieving anti-inflammatory and fever alleviating drug. It belongs to the group of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory group of drugs (NSAIDs).3,4

No water is needed when taking the drug, it is ready to use and each dose comes in its own sachet stick.1 Each sachet of oral solution contains 25 mg of dexketoprofen.It is a colourless solution with a sweet flavour and citric-lemon smell.3 Each 25 mg dose comes in its own sachet, which makes it also convenient to use on the go.

WHY IS DOLMEN® 25 LIQUID STICK UNIQUE?


ABOUT THE DRUG

01

NO WATER NEEDED - READY TO USE

Convenient to use when acute pain occurs suddenly while away from home (e.g., at work, when travelling) where it may not be possible take a tablet with water or dissolve granules in a glass of water

02

FAST ONSET OF ACTION:

It starts to work rapidly. The oral solution takes 0.33 h (=~20 minutes) to reach peak plasma concentrations (tmax) whereas a tablet needs 30 minutes.2,3
